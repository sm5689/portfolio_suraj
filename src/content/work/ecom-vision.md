### Project Overview
Engineered a comprehensive dashboard to provide business intelligence insights for e-commerce store owners. The platform aggregates raw sales data into actionable metrics through interactive charts and graphs.

### Key Features
- **Visualization**: Delivered 8 interactive visualizations across 4 business metrics using D3.js or Recharts.
- **Optimization**: Refined Redux Toolkit state management, reducing unnecessary re-renders by 45%.
- **Data Pipeline**: Established MongoDB aggregation pipeline with 6 optimized queries, achieving 30% faster data retrieval.
- **Performance**: Improved Time-to-Interactive from 2.1s to 1.6s.
