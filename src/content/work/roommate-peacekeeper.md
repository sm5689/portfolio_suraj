### Project Overview
Built to solve the common friction points of shared living, Roommate Peacekeeper serves as a central hub for household responsibilities. It focuses on transparency and ease of use to ensure equitable distribution of chores and bills.

### Key Features
- **State Management**: Focused on clean state management for real-time updates across users.
- **UI Workflows**: Designed intuitive workflows for task assignment and expense tracking.
- **Signal**: Curated for systems thinking, performance, and clean engineering.
